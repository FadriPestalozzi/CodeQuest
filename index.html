About Hackergarten Events

hack-commit-push is a series of events inviting attendees to start contributing to Free/Open Source projects with the help of their maintainers. The event is open to everyone, regardless of their experience. Every contribution counts!

Protips for Project Leads

How to Become One

You do not need to be the owner of an open-source project to be accepted as a project lead. However, you must contribute regularly and be knowledgeable about the entire codebase.

Before the Event Starts

Promote the event and your project to attract contributors! Prepare a presentation or video explaining your project.

During the Event

Welcome contributors and provide general information. Split into smaller groups for collaboration.

After the Event

Send a recap of the contributions to the organizers.

Protips for Contributors

Do I Qualify?

Short answer: yes, you do! Everyone is welcome, regardless of experience.

Before the Event Starts

Share the event with friends and prepare by reading project documentation.

During the Event

Attend training sessions and collaborate with others.

After the Event

If you liked the experience, feel free to continue your contributions!

Code of Conduct

Our events are meant to be a harassment-free experience for everyone. We will not tolerate harassment of participants in any form.
